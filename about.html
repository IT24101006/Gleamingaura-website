Who We Are

Gem Island Artisans

Gleaming Aura Gems & Jewellery was created from a deep passion for Sri Lanka's world-renowned gemstones and fine jewellery craftsmanship. Built on decades of experience in the gem trade, we carefully source high-quality natural and heated gemstones, including sapphires, rubies, alexandrite, and other precious stones from the Gem Island of Sri Lanka.

We combine these exceptional gems with elegant jewellery designs to create pieces that celebrate beauty, individuality, and timeless style. Every gemstone is selected with care, and every jewellery piece is crafted to highlight its natural brilliance.

From stunning ready-to-wear jewellery to personalized custom creations, our goal is to provide our customers with authentic gemstones, exceptional craftsmanship, and a memorable experience from selection to delivery.

At Gleaming Aura, we believe every gem has a story — and we are here to help you make it yours. 💎✨

What We Stand For

Our Principles

🌍

Ethical Sourcing

Every gem is traced to its origin. We work only with licensed Sri Lankan miners and dealers committed to fair trade and responsible extraction.

✋

True Handcraft

No factory machines. Each piece is shaped, set, and polished by the hands of experienced artisans who have spent decades perfecting their craft.

💯

Transparent Quality

We provide full disclosure on every gem — natural or heated, carat weight, colour grade, and origin. What you see is what you receive.
