FAQ

Common Questions

How long does a custom order take? +

Most custom pieces are completed within 2–4 weeks, depending on complexity. We'll give you an exact timeframe when you reach out. Rush orders may be possible — just ask.

What's the difference between natural and heated gems? +

Natural gems are untreated and exactly as mined. Heated gems have been gently heat-treated to enhance colour and clarity — an industry-standard process that doesn't affect durability. We disclose all treatment details upfront.

Do you ship worldwide? +

Yes! We ship to 40+ countries with full insurance. Shipping typically takes 7–21 business days depending on your location. Tracking is provided on every order.

Can I see the gem before committing? +

Absolutely. Once we source a gem for your order, we'll send detailed photos and videos before setting it. You can approve or request alternatives — no pressure at all.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept bank transfers, PayPal, Western Union, and most major card payments. A 50% deposit is taken to begin your order, with the balance due before shipping.
